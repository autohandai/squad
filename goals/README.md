## Product Thesis

Autohand Squad should become less "chat with agents" and more "operate a small AI software team with evidence, memory, permissions, and workflows."

The differentiator is trust infrastructure: the app should show what each agent knew, what it did, why it did it, whether it worked, and what risk remains.
