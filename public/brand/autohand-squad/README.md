# Autohand Squad Brand Assets

The GPT-generated source mark is archived in `source/autohand-squad-gpt-master.png`.

- Root assets: dark and light PNG/WebP icons plus transparent marks.
- `icons/`: PNG/WebP exports at 16, 32, 48, 64, 128, 180, 192, 256, 512, and 1024 px for both dark and light themes.
- `social/`: avatar, circular avatar, square, Open Graph, X/Twitter, LinkedIn, YouTube, and story formats in PNG and WebP.
- `lockups/`: horizontal wordmark lockups on dark, light, and transparent backgrounds.
